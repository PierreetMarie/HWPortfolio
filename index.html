About me

My name is Enrique Sandino and I was born in Nicaragua. Nicaragua is located in Central America and it is a country with no more than 7 million habitants. I studied in a private school and I was able to learn English and French there. I consider myself a person that enjoys playing sports. In highschool I was captain of the soccer team and eventually I worked myself up to play in Division I for soccer.

My highschool was really artsy and I learned how to play the guitar and the violin. During my highschool days my friends and I form a band and started playing in different concerts. Sadly my best friends ended up moving to different countries and the band broke up.
